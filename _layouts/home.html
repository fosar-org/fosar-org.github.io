Family of Shipyard Assisting Robotics


A Force Multiplier

The Family of Shipyard Assisting Robotics (FOSAR) initiative represents a groundbreaking approach to advancing shipyard automation and robotics. Designed to address the growing complexity of naval sustainment while fostering collaboration across the supply chain, FOSAR is positioned to play a transformative role in shipyard operations in 2025. By leveraging modular, scalable, and flexible technologies, FOSAR promotes innovation while ensuring compatibility across diverse platforms and applications.

National Initiative

FOSAR operates as a national-level initiative, bringing together Boston Engineering and other partners to create common tools and capabilities under a unified platform. By fostering commonality of design and operation, utilizing open software development, and integrating advanced sensors and communications equipment, FOSAR optimizes sustainment efforts.

Connected, Future-Facing Capabilities

The inclusion of digital tools for data visualization and network connectivity further enhances the overall solution, creating a comprehensive and forward-looking approach to both sustainment and robotics.
